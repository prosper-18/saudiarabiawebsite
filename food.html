Saudi Arabia is the largest country in the Middle East and is known as the birthplace of Islam, home to the holy cities of Mecca and Medina. The country has a monarchy, and its economy is heavily based on oil, being one of the world's leading oil exporters. Saudi Arabia is undergoing significant modernization through its Vision 2030 plan, aiming to diversify its economy and reduce reliance on oil.
